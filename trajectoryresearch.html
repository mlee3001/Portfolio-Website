Trajectory Dynamics Research

GitHub Repository Link

This research is focused on the study of interaction kernels for interaction dynamics. The paper "Nonparametric inference of interaction laws in systems of agents from trajectory data", by Fei Lu et al, was primarily used as a basis for this research. Using algorithms to compute learning matricies and solve linear systems in order to investigate the potential source interaction kernel for a dynamic system. The links to the paper and associated appendix can be found below.

The method used for simulation and the subsequent learning of the source interaction kernel is dimensionally independent and thus the 1 dimensional and 2 dimensional simulations are shown here to represent that.
